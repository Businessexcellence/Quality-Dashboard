## **DELIVERY TIPS**

### **Pace:**
- Speak at 140-160 words per minute (normal conversational pace)
- Slow down for key numbers (ROI, CSAT decline)
- Speed up slightly for background/context

### **Emphasis:**
- **Bold words** above = vocal emphasis
- *Italics* above = actions to take (clicking, pausing)
- CAPS = significant emphasis

### **Pauses:**
- After "5.13 out of 7" - pause 3 seconds
- After asking if they're ready to transform - pause until they respond
- After any major number - pause 1-2 seconds

### **Body Language:**
- Stand (don't sit)
- Make eye contact with both CEO and CHRO alternately
- Use hand gestures for emphasis
- Move closer during the CSAT crisis reveal

### **Energy:**
- Start at 7/10 energy
- Build to 9/10 for the CSAT crisis reveal
- Stay at 8/10 for ROI and actions
- End at 9/10 for the closing ask
