## **PRESENTATION TIPS**

**Delivery Guidelines:**

1. **Confidence & Authority**: You're presenting solutions, not problems. Speak with conviction.

2. **Pause for Impact**: After showing the CSAT decline, pause 3-4 seconds. Let it sink in.

3. **Eye Contact**: Alternate between CEO and CHRO. Watch for reactions.

4. **Use the Dashboard Live**: Don't just show slides - actually navigate the dashboard. It's impressive.

5. **Connect to Their Goals**: 
   - CEO cares about: Revenue, growth, competitive position
   - CHRO cares about: People performance, client satisfaction, operational excellence

6. **Tell Stories, Not Just Numbers**:
   - "Last month, we caught a quality issue 5 days earlier than we would have..."
   - "One recruiter improved accuracy by 12% after seeing their performance data..."

7. **Be Ready to Go Deeper**: If they want to drill into any section, be prepared to navigate live.

8. **End Strong**: Your closing statement should be memorable and actionable.

**Body Language:**
- Stand, don't sit (if presenting in person)
- Use hand gestures to emphasize key points
- Move around the room (don't stay static)
- Smile when discussing wins and opportunities

**Voice Modulation:**
- Slow down for critical numbers (ROI, CSAT decline)
- Speed up slightly for background/context
- Use emphasis on action words (NOW, URGENT, CRITICAL)

**Timing Control:**
- Slides 1-3: 2.5 min (set context)
- Slides 4-5: 3 min (show capabilities)
- Slide 6: 2 min (live demo - the wow moment)
- Slides 7-8: 2.5 min (business case)
- Slides 9-10: 2 min (close and ask)
- **Total: 10 minutes**
- **Q&A: 5-10 minutes additional**

**What to Bring:**
- Laptop with dashboard open (primary)
- Backup: Slides with screenshots
- Printed executive summary (2 copies)
- Calculator (for ROI questions)
- Notebook for action items

**Room Setup:**
- Test screen sharing 30 min before
- Have dashboard already logged in
- Test internet connection
- Ensure audio works if remote
